    Why CARD.IO Wellness?

    Our holistic integrative approach is designed to tune in to the physical, emotional and spiritual levels, allowing an organic healing process to naturally unfold. Our overall aim is to co-create a trusting connection and gently guide you through a highly personalised programme, while providing you with useful life tools to return home with.

    Mission

    We are committed to providing you with an exceptional experience from the moment you walk through our door. We take the time to listen to you and help you evaluate all of your options. We are here to empower you to look and feel your “natural” best through our high level of expertise and high standards. We look forward to building a trusting relationship with you.

    First Visit

    Please arrive half an hour early to your appointment to fill in the registration and medical history paperwork. Alternatively, you can download and fill in the forms prior to your appointment and bring them with you.

    Consultation Fee

    Consultations are one hour in length and include a full aesthetic evaluation, a full skin analysis using a woods lamp and a customized home skincare programme. Normally a consultation has a fee associated with it. However,your first one hour consultation is free of charge.

    Cancellation Policy

    We respect the value of your time and ask the same of you. Therefore, we request the courtesy of a 24 hour advanced notice for rescheduling or canceling your appointment. If this request is not honored, a $125.00 fee will be incurred and your credit card may be used to book future appointments.
